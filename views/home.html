Welcome to the NBA Centre


This is just a small project to read data from json files. Then sending them to the server.

All the data will be displayed in JSON format.


Some of the functionalities:

To see all the players, navigate to http://localhost:8080/Players or click on "Players" on the upper left corner

To see all the teams, navigate to http://localhost:8080/Teams or click on "Teams" on the upper left corner

To search player by last name, navigate to http://localhost:8080/Players/:lastname

To search team by name, navigate to http://localhost:8080/Teams/:teamname

To search team by location, navigate to http://localhost:8080/Teams/:location


Note: :location, :teamname, :lastname are the inputs, replace them with the names you are looking for.
